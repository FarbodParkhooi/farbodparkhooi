## 👨‍💻 About Me

<img align="right" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/qgQUggAC3Pfv687qPC/giphy.gif" width="250" alt="Coding gif" />

I'm a **professional programmer** based in Iran with a passion for building things from the ground up:

- 🧠 **Core Languages**: Python, C, C++ — and proficient across many more  
- 🤖 **Specialties**: AI/ML, Computer Vision, Embedded Systems, Microcontroller Programming  
- 🛠️ **Hardware Design**: Professional SolidWorks (3D/mechanical) & Altium (PCB/electronics)  
- ⚙️ **Philosophy**: No black boxes. I build from scratch to understand every layer.

🔭 **Currently crafting**:
- **Agaah Counseling Center** — Django booking system, multi-role, Persian UI, video sessions  
- **DIY AR Goggles** — Video See-Through headset with custom PCB and computer vision  
- **CIFAR-10 GAN & cGAN** — Generative models in PyTorch  
- **F35-view** — A simulated F35 HUD with CV-powered object targeting

🌱 **Learning**: Advanced GANs, TTS/voice generation, cutting-edge computer vision

💬 **Ask me about**: AI/ML, embedded systems, PCB design, 3D printing, or building without shortcuts

⚡ **Fun fact**: I designed a custom STM32F103C8T6 flight controller from scratch — my own firmware, no Betaflight.
